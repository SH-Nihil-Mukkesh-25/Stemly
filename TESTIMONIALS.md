## Share Your Experience

We'd love to feature your feedback here. You can:

1. **Open a GitHub issue** with the title `[TESTIMONIAL] Your feedback`
2. **Email us** at stemly.team@gmail.com
3. **Submit a PR** adding your testimonial directly to this file

### Template

```markdown
> "Your experience with Stemly — what you used it for, what you liked,
> what could be improved."
>
> — **Your Name**, Role/Year, Institution (optional)
```

### Guidelines

- Be honest — constructive criticism is welcome
- Mention specific features you found useful (or not)
- Include your institution/role only if you're comfortable
- We may lightly edit for clarity, but will always keep your meaning
